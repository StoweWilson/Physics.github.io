Understanding Kinematic
What is it?
Kinematic is the study of motion without considering the forces that cause it.It focuses on displacement, velocity, acceleration, and time.
Key Concepts
  • Displacement (Δx): The change in position of an object.
  • Velocity (v): The rate of change of displacement.
  • Acceleration (a): The rate of change of velocity.
  • Time (t): The duration of motion.
Kinematic Equations

1. Final Velocity:

v = vi + a * t

2. Displacement:

Δx = vi * t + 0.5 * a * t²

3. Final Velocity Squared:

v² = vi² + 2 * a * Δx

4. Average Velocity:

vavg = (v + vi) / 2
